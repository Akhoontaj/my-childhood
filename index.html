Qeema Moutter Recipe

Now we are going to make Qeema mutter Recipe

    ingredients

  • Beef 1/2 kg
  • mutter 1/2 kg
  • mutter 1/2 kg
  • oil 300 grm
  • onion 250 grm
  • tomato 250 grm
  • Dhaniya 100 grm
  • garam masala 100 grm
  • chilli need basis
  • Salt need basis

Method

  1. First of all wash the qeema & mutter
  2. grind the onion, tomato, dhaniya, chillietc and make the paste from them
  3. put the frypan on the burner and put the oil in it
  4. put the paste in the hot oil
  5. mix it with the spoon
  6. then put the qeema and mutter in it
  7. mix it with the oil and paste properly
  8. keep it on the burner for 20 minuts
  9. yes my recipe is ready
